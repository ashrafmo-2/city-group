About City Group

Building the future of business through excellence, innovation, and unwavering commitment to corporate values.

Who We Are

City Group is a leading conglomerate based in Kuwait, with a diverse portfolio spanning real estate, investments, trading, and logistics. Since our founding, we have been committed to delivering exceptional value to our clients, partners, and stakeholders.

With decades of market experience, we have established a reputation for excellence, innovation, and reliability. Our affiliate companies operate synergistically to provide integrated solutions that meet the evolving needs of the regional market.

We take pride in our legacy of sustainable growth, corporate responsibility, and positive contribution to the communities we serve. Our team of experienced professionals leads every initiative with integrity, vision, and a commitment to excellence.

Vision

To be the leading regional group recognized for sustainable growth, operational excellence, and creating lasting value for all stakeholders.

Mission

To deliver innovative solutions across our diverse portfolio while maintaining the highest standards of quality, integrity, and corporate responsibility.

Values

Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, Collaboration, and Accountability - these pillars guide every decision we make and every relationship we build.

Our Strategy

Our business strategy is built on four core pillars that guide our growth and ensure continued success in a dynamic business environment.

Sustainable Growth

Pursuing strategic opportunities that deliver long-term value while maintaining financial prudence and operational excellence.

Innovation & Adaptation

Embracing new technologies and business models to stay ahead in a rapidly changing market environment.

Talent Development

Investing in our people through continuous training, development programs, and creating pathways for career advancement.

Regional Expansion

Strategically expanding our footprint across the Gulf region while maintaining our commitment to quality and service.
